Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Number Leader

Number Leader is a comprehensive platform designed to connect startups, investors, and enablers in the ecosystem. We provide AI-powered tools, curated deal flow, and tailored strategies to help startups grow, investors find opportunities, and enablers expand their impact.

We offer startups a comprehensive suite of tools including:

  • FP&A Suite for managing actuals and streamlining planning, budgeting, and forecasting
  • Fund Readiness Suite for valuation tools, AI-powered reports, business plans, and pitch decks
  • Fund Raising Suite to connect with the right investors and manage fundraising pipelines

Investors gain access to:

  • Private Market Intelligence Platform to explore startups by sector with key financials
  • Investor Tool Suite with CRM to find promising startups aligned with investment thesis
  • Fund Raising Suite for funds seeking capital from angel investors and LPs

Enablers can leverage our platform to:

  • List services, display success stories, and share startup pitches on our Showcasing Platform
  • Use our Startup Acquisition Suite with CRM to identify high-potential startups
  • Expand investor networks with our Investor Acquisition Suite

Getting started is simple:

  1. Visit our website and click on the appropriate button to join as a startup, investor, or enabler
  2. Create your account and complete your profile
  3. Explore the platform and start using our tools tailored to your needs
  4. Connect with other members of the ecosystem

For any assistance, you can contact our support team at info@numberleader.com
